BulkBox

A food delivery platform connecting foodies with authentic Japanese restaurants

  1. Home
  2. Work
  3. BulkBox

Our objectives

  • Due to the abundant Japanese restaurants operating in Saigon, Vietnam, the aim is to connect owners with customers by offering delivery services.
  • Facilitate the accessibility of Japanese cuisine and imported products from Japan to local customers through a delivery application.

Project info

MY ROLE
  • UI/UX designer
  • Developed email templates
  • 1 designer, 4 developers, 1 tester, and 1 project manager
  • Year: 2020

Result

  • Bulkbox was acquired by my company after being released to customers.
  • Subsequently, Bulkbox underwent a redesign and rebranding, transforming into Eaty.
  • Eaty has been operational on both the App Store and Google Play for 1 year.

  • Select one category.
